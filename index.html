How Fishing Is Polluting Our Oceans

In excess of 100 million pounds of plastic from modern fishing gear contaminate the seas every year-undermining marine life. ... An expected 4.6 million fishing vessels utilize sea waters, setting fishing gear across each sea bowl. Fishing tools can be lost unintentionally deserted adrift. When there nets and lines can represent a danger to untamed life for years or many years, trapping everything from little fish and shellfish to imperiled turtles, seabirds and even whales,' the report says. "Spreading all through the sea on tides and flows, lost and disposed of fishing gear is presently floating to Arctic shorelines, appearing on far off Pacific Islands, snared on coral reefs and littering the remote ocean floor.
